Our Journey

From Crisis to Care, From Vision to Value

What began during the COVID-19 pandemic as a mission to serve patients in underserved rural areas, soon evolved into a movement. HealthCRAD emerged in 2022, driven by the real stories of rural patients and the untapped strength of local medicine stores — the unsung heroes during the healthcare crisis.

With passion in our purpose, we served 3000+ patients, 83+ B2B orders and created a growing ecosystem that now connects 1500+ pharmacy retailers, 100+ doctors, 50+ ambulances, and verified medicine suppliers — all working in unison to transform rural healthcare access.

From a spark of service to a nationwide vision, HealthCRAD has become a name trusted by thousands. And now, with HealthCRAD Wholesale, we’re bringing a game-changing platform exclusively for our pharmacies — empowering them with bulk medicine procurement, AI-backed tools, flexible payments, and unmatched earnings.

This is just the beginning. Join us as we continue to reshape healthcare, from the grassroots up. Be a part of Rural Healthcare Revolution
